What are the signs of depression?

Research shows that 1 out of 16 people in Australia suffer from depression.And even in these times of a global pandemic, these numbers have risen, as people struggle to navigate these increasingly tough and difficult times.

But the truth is that you don't have to live with depression. It may be possible to make a change - and it starts with you understanding the signs and symptoms of depression.

So, what are some of the signs of depression that you need to be aware of;

  • Constantly feeling down and moody
  • Loss of interest and pleasure in performing your usual activities
  • Changes in appetite or weight
  • Insomnia (lack of sleep)
  • Fatigue or constant loss of energy
  • Feelings of worthlessness or guilt
  • Have difficulty in concentrating
