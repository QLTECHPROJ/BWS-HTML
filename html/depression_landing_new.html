How Brain Wellness Spa works

Our signature program is the Emotional Empowerment Program, which consists of 14 sessions. The first ten essions address all of your triggers and the next four sessions work on your sabotaging patterns of behaviour. The effectiveness of this process involves an engagement with the unconscious part of the brain, through the use of positive auditory stimuli, a technique that attempts to work on the unconscious brain (your automatic brain), whilst you are in a state of relaxation throughout your mental wellness session.

At the Brain Wellness Spa, we also aim to help people get into recovery sleep. Its helpful to understand that falling into bed exhausted and waking up exhausted, is a good indicator that you have not accessed recovery sleep.up in the morning.

Before Brenda came to Transformational Wellness she was suffering with clinical depression and drinking heavily. After her first visit with Transformational Wellness, Brenda felt peaceful and her negativity was gone. Brenda is now living a much happier life and has been alcohol free for three years now.
